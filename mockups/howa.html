What HoWA does

Three capabilities that work together. Remembering is the foundation. Guiding is the intelligence. Protecting is the promise.

Remembers

Your home's records, warranties, maintenance history, seasonal rhythms, provider relationships, and the preferences that make your household yours.

Guides

Recommendations, service scheduling, design direction, and seasonal prompts. Not reminders. Guidance that learns from how your home actually lives.

Protects

Risk review, insurance connected to real care history, evidence and verification, and a protection record that grows over time.
